About The Symposium

The Conference of Religious Women India (CRWI), in collaboration with the Global Research Network (GRN), is delighted to invite you to this Symposium.

For centuries, consecrated women have embodied Christ’s tenderness through ministries of education, pastoral care, healthcare, formation, social outreach, eldercare, ecological stewardship, and media engagement. Their fidelity and service continue to illuminate challenging realities with courage, creativity, and unwavering hope.

This Symposium will offer a platform for sharing research insights, personal narratives, and collaborative reflections on the evolving mission of Consecrated Women amid contemporary global changes.

- Pope Francis

Call for Papers

We invite submissions on the thematic areas listed above. Rooted in the spirit of a Pilgrimage of Hope, this gathering seeks to deepen communion and foster renewed commitment.

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts: 300–400 words (title, theme, objectives, methodology, keywords).
  • Full Papers: 4,000–6,000 words (APA style).
  • Bio Note: 150 words with affiliation.
  • Language: English.
