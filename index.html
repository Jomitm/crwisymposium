About The Symposium

The Conference of Religious Women India (CRWI), in collaboration with the Global Research Network (GRN), is delighted to invite you to this Symposium.

For centuries, consecrated women have embodied Christ‚Äôs tenderness through ministries of education, pastoral care, healthcare, formation, social outreach, eldercare, ecological stewardship, and media engagement. Their fidelity and service continue to illuminate challenging realities with courage, creativity, and unwavering hope.
